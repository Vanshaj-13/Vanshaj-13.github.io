I’m Vanshaj Malhotra — a robotics engineer, systems thinker, and relentless builder of machines that push the boundaries of possibility. With a foundation in mechanical engineering and a specialization in robotics from the University of Toronto, I blend engineering precision with visionary design to create unmanned systems that thrive in environments where humans cannot. From designing Mars rovers and aerial systems for interplanetary exploration, to building real-time autonomous drone racers and ADAS solutions for mine safety, my work spans the cutting edge of aerospace, AI, and embedded systems. My journey includes leading 50+ engineers in global competitions, publishing peer-reviewed research, and developing scalable prototypes in both industry and academia. Whether it’s a self-balancing wheelchair, a portable UAV launcher, or a neural-inspired path planner — I don’t just prototype ideas. I launch them.
